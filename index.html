The Problem
  • Institutional campuses (hostels, academic blocks) lose enormous volumes of freshwater to hidden pipe ruptures and tank overflows.
  • Management is reactive — leaks surface only after a bill spike or a visible flood, long after damage begins.
  • Fixed threshold alarms fail: they miss slow leaks and drown staff in false alerts.
💧
Our Solution
  • Hardware-Agnostic Ingestion: Connects to off-the-shelf smart meters or existing campus SCADA telemetry.
  • WaterSense AI Engine: A Random Forest model learns time-of-day baselines and flags anomalies at >90% confidence (F1: 89.8%, ROC-AUC: 0.99).
  • Confirmed leaks trigger instant alerts to maintenance — shrinking response time from days to minutes.

Data Ingestion Layer (Hardware Agnostic)
  • Standard Sensor Compatibility: Ingests data from standard pulse flow sensors (YF-S201) & ultrasonic tank gauges (HC-SR04).
  • No Custom Hardware Required: Plugs directly into existing campus Wi-Fi/LoRa gateways or third-party IoT controllers (ESP32/Raspberry Pi).
  • Multi-Protocol Support: Lightweight REST APIs & MQTT telemetry streams for seamless, vendor-neutral data collection.
  • Edge Pre-Processing: Real-time filtering of sensor noise and transmission dropouts before cloud ingestion.
☁️
Core Software & AI Stack
  • Cloud Data Pipeline: Firebase Realtime Database & InfluxDB time-series buffering for high-throughput sensor telemetry.
  • Machine Learning Engine: Python-based scikit-learn Random Forest model trained on 12 key rolling flow & pressure features.
  • Real-Time Visualization: Interactive Streamlit dashboard displaying live campus leak risk heatmaps and pressure z-scores.
  • Automated Dispatch: Twilio SMS & email alert integration with severity grading and location pinpointing.

What Worked
  • Pressure deviation emerged as top feature (37.6% importance), validating physics-based intuition
  • Flow z-score (20%) + pressure (24%) together drive 82% of detections — robust signal
  • 0.1% false positive rate — only 23 false alarms out of 22,735 normal test samples
  • Model correctly identified all 20 injected leak scenarios across pipe ruptures, slow leaks, and overflows
⚡
Lessons Learned
  • Wi-Fi coverage gaps caused intermittent data loss in Hostel Block C
  • Flow sensor calibration drift after 10 days — needs periodic recalibration
  • Single cloud endpoint created latency spikes during peak data ingestion
  • Staff training needed: 40% of alerts were acknowledged but not acted upon

Risk Management

Risk Assessment & Mitigation

Proactive identification and mitigation strategies for scaling challenges

🔴
Network Connectivity Failures
Large-scale Wi-Fi/LoRa outages could blind the system during critical leak events
Dual-mode connectivity (Wi-Fi + LoRa), 72-hour edge buffering, mesh network redundancy
🔴
Sensor Degradation & Drift
Flow sensors lose accuracy over time due to mineral deposits and mechanical wear
Automated drift detection via ML, scheduled quarterly recalibration, self-test routines
🟠
Cloud Service Costs Escalation
Data ingestion costs could spike unpredictably at 2,000+ node scale
Edge pre-processing reduces cloud data by 70%, tiered storage with cold archival, cost alerts
🟠
Adoption Resistance
Maintenance staff may resist new workflows or ignore system alerts
Phased training program, gamified alert response KPIs, management buy-in from day 1
🟠
Data Privacy & Security
IoT endpoints are vulnerable to unauthorized access and data interception
TLS encryption end-to-end, device authentication via X.509 certificates, network segmentation
🟢
Funding Gaps Between Phases
University budget cycles may delay procurement for Phase 2/3
Phased ROI reports to justify funding, apply for AICTE/DST grants, explore PPP models
